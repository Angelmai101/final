About Us

Our Mission and Vision

The purpose of the Equality is to provide information and resources related to the work and mission of the organization. The website serves as a platform for the Equality to communicate with the public and raise awareness about its efforts to promote peace, human rights, and sustainable development around the world. Visitors to the site can access a wealth of information, including news updates, reports, publications, and multimedia content on a wide range of topics, from gender inequality and poverty to international law and conflict resolution. The website also provides resources for educators, researchers, and policymakers to engage with the Equality’s work and promote global cooperation and understanding.

The Equality is an autonomous body of the Mindx, established to contribute to and strengthen the promotion of gender equality, including gender mainstreaming in all world’s policies and the resulting national policies, and the fight against discrimination based on sex, as well as to raise world citizens’ awareness of gender equality.

Our Team

Equality is a fundamental principle that recognizes that all individuals should have the same rights, opportunities, and treatment regardless of their background, gender, race, religion, or any other personal characteristic. It means that everyone should be treated with fairness and impartiality, and no one should be discriminated against or disadvantaged because of their differences. Achieving equality requires a commitment to social justice and a willingness to challenge unjust systems and practices that perpetuate inequality. Ultimately, creating a more equal society benefits everyone by promoting diversity, tolerance, and respect for human dignity.

Our team is made up of experienced professionals who are dedicated to delivering the best possible experience for our customers. We work together to ensure that every project is completed to the highest standard.

About The Author

Who is she?

Nguyen Ngoc Mai is the author behind the Equality. She is student from American International School of VietNam. Her work across multiple disciplines broadly addresses narratives of gender equality. As a student, Nguyen Ngoc Mai has a passion for mathematics and coding. His work has appeared on Mindx.

Why did she create this website?

Gender equality is important because it is a fundamental human right and a crucial element of social justice. It ensures that all individuals, regardless of their gender identity, have equal access to opportunities, resources, and power. When gender inequality exists, it can lead to a wide range of social, economic, and political problems, such as discrimination, violence, poverty, and exclusion. There are problems with gender inequality that Nguyen Ngoc Mai wants to share with the global citizens so that we could improve the world and make it better. Sharing information about gender equality that happens around the world could help to build this world a better place.
